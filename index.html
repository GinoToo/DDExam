How to Use HDMI Five

1

Allow Camera Access

When you first visit the site, grant camera permission to enable scanning functionality.

2

Position Your Document

Place the exam paper on a flat surface with good lighting. Make sure all four corners are visible within the scan frame.

3

Capture the Image

Tap the circular capture button to take a photo. Hold your device steady for the best results.

4

Review and Process

Check the preview and tap "Process & Save PDF" to extract the exam paper and generate a printable PDF.

5

Access Your Scans

View all your scanned exam papers in the History section. You can download, print, or share them anytime.
